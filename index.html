"India is the nation of villages and the soul of India lives in its villages."
This statement by Mahatma Gandhi has become outdated now, because it is nearly immpossible to find a pure handmade craft made by the villagers. We at "Buy And Sell" provide you the facility to reach out to the original handmade crafts.

Handicrafts may be used in every fields.
Some of them are :

Bamboo Products

These are one of the most important and useful handicrafts used by almost every Indian. In villages bamboo products are used for storing things, carrying and cleaning houses. In bamboo products we provide baskets, brooms, soops, etc.

Pottery

In India about 70% people are not economically able to buy a refrigerator. They use handmade pots to get cold water in the summer season and now-a-days it is the demand of time ot stop using refrigerators as it affects the ecosystem.
Handmade statues are used in every corner of the country for worshiping in the festivals. We provide finest pottery and beautiful statues.

Home Decor

Either it is a family funtion time or a social gathering or any festival decoration is done heartily to make houses look beautiful. We provide handmade attractive "Jhalar's(Toran)" to decorate houses.
